It's an emulator, innit?

About

GBCC is a cross-platform Game Boy and Game Boy Color emulator written in C, with a focus on accuracy. Available on Linux, macOS, Windows, and Android.

Features

Emulator

  • SDL & GTK GUIs
  • Shaders: Dot-Matrix, Colour-Correct and Subpixel
    • Colour-Correct and Subpixel shaders provide accurate GBC colour reproduction
  • Screenshots, both with & without shaders applied
  • Gamepad support
  • Cartridge saves, including real-time clock data
    • Auto-save after game writes to SRAM
  • Save states
    • Auto-save on close & resume on open
  • Adjustable turbo mode
  • Palettes for DMG games
  • Frame blending
  • VRAM tile data display
